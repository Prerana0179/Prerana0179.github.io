About Me

I design with
intention

I'm a UI/UX Designer based in Bangalore with hands-on experience designing platform modules at TatvaOps — a product-first startup in the interior design space. In 4+ months, I've taken multiple platform modules from zero to fully polished, interactive prototypes.

I believe great design is invisible — it solves problems so elegantly that users don't notice the craft, only the ease. I work at the intersection of visual beauty and deep usability, bringing systems thinking to every screen I touch.

When I'm not in Figma, I'm exploring motion design, obsessing over micro-interactions, or building things on Wix Studio just to see what's possible.
